Our Journey

How It All Began

October 12, 2021

First Coffee & Coincidence

It started with a spilled macchiato at a small Parisian cafe. A clumsy bump, a quick apology, and a conversation that lasted four hours until the cafe closed. We knew then that something special was brewing.

August 24, 2023

Our First Home Away

Traveling across Italy, we got lost in the cobblestone streets of Florence. Under a sudden summer storm, we took shelter under a balcony, sharing a single slice of pizza. That's when we first said "I love you."

December 24, 2025

The Proposal on the Seine

On a freezing Christmas Eve, Nithin booked a private boat ride on the Seine. Right in front of the glittering Eiffel Tower, he dropped to one knee. Amala said yes before he could even finish the question.
